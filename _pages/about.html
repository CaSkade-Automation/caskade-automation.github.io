About CaSkade

CaSkade is a framework that summarizes a series of actvities in the field of capability and skill-based manufacturing. Over the past five years, scientists of the Institute of Automation Technology at the Helmut Schmidt University / University of the Federal Armed Forces Hamburg have researched models and methods, including the associated software solutions, and implemented them in several projects.
